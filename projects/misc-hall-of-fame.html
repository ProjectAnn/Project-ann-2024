Misc Hall of Fame

The Stranger

The University of Auckland | Aug 2015 | Graphic design - Poster, booklet

I collaborated with the curatorial team to design promotional materials for the university art exhibition The Stranger. My work included creating a poster and booklet that showcased the artists and engaged the audience with the exhibition's thought-provoking theme. The concept explored the idea of thresholds—where we encounter others and ourselves.

*confused screaming*

Final year project for BFA | Sep 2017 | 3D Animation, installation

My final year project at the University of Auckland was a 3D animation and installation exploring themes of utopia, dystopia, and the intersections between urban environments and digital spaces. The animation is set in a minimalist cityscape, using contrasting colours and abstract forms to create an otherworldly atmosphere, highlighting how architecture and technology shape human interaction. I aimed to challenge the viewers' understanding of an idealized future, questioning whether such visions are achievable or inherently flawed.

NZCO

Fonterra Ventures | Mar 2017 | Graphic design, UI design

As part of my work with Fonterra Ventures, I collaborated on visualizing innovative business models to enhance B2B sales. One project was NZCO, a platform designed to streamline the ordering process for small businesses such as cafes. The platform offered a simple and intuitive interface for ordering Fonterra products, like protein powders and other ingredients, allowing customers to enhance their menu. My role involved creating visual concepts that clearly communicated the platform value proposition while ensuring a seamless user experience.

Ryman Quests

Ryman Healthcare | Dec 2021 | Hackathon - UX/UI design

Ryman Quests was a gamified solution developed during a 2-day hackathon at Ryman, aimed at encouraging residents to increase their physical activity levels. The concept integrated engaging quests and challenges into residents' daily routines, rewarding them for completing tasks such as walking, stretching, or participating in group activities. My team placed 3rd out of 24 teams!
